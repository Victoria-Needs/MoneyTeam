COVID ASSISTANCE

The COVID 19 pandemic has been a tragedy that has effected millions globally. One way to look at this issue from a positive point of view is that during these lockdowns, lots of money can be saved.

Victoria has recorded 200 days of lockdown over 6 lockdowns. This puts the average length of a lockdown to just over a month.

According to Finder AU, the average person saves approximately $718 monthly pre COVID.

The Australian Bureau of Statistics have found that incomes and savings have risen by at least 21% since June 2021, meaning that at a minimum, savings monthly should have increased to $800.

This calculator will work out the average amount of money saved in lockdown and using that information will provide specific tips and tricks to help assist you financially during these tough unforsaken times.
